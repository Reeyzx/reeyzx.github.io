# Description

My digital artifact consists of 3 interconnected components created for the Roblox raycast vehicle community: a playable demo experience on Roblox, an unlisted YouTube showcase video, and a Bluesky announcement post published November 7, 2025.

Together, these artifacts document my shift from observer to creator within this developer niche, shaping up as a major part of my ethnographic research process.
