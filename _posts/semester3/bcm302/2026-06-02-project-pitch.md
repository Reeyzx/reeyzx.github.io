# Overview

Our project revolves around the theme of **overconsumption** and we have decided to create a visual novel based off of this. We have also put consideration to incorporate interactive elements to broaden appeal to those who are not necessarily familiar regarding visual novels. Specific examples include quick time events (QTE) and gacha simulation throughout the narrative to provide tactile feedback.

The game is set in a world where overconsumption has taken over, and the player can learn about the effects it had through dialogue and interactions. Instead of playing as a "consumer" character, the player will take on the role of an unwanted teddy bear to explore the nuance via the lens of a "commodity".

The purpose of our project is to **raise awareness** as well as **educate** people from our targeted age group (18-25) about the dangers of overconsumption and what they can do to help or prevent it, in the tone of a darkly humorous cautionary tale.

# Research

Our project is informed by specific research from **McKinsey & Company.**This piece outlines the phenomenon of "doom spending" among Gen Z and millennials, showing purchasing as a coping mechanism despite lower relative wealth.
